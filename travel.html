why visit Asmara ?

Asmara is a city where history, culture, and modernity meet. Its unique Art Deco architecture, historical landmarks, and vibrant local culture make it a must-visit destination for travelers seeking a blend of tradition and modernity. Experience the charm of Asmara’s streets, savor local cuisine, and enjoy the hospitality of its people.

When to Visit

The best time to visit Asmara is during the cooler months, from October to March, when temperatures are mild and pleasant. During this time, the weather is ideal for exploring the city on foot and enjoying outdoor activities.

Travel Tips

Currency

The Eritrean Nakfa (ERN) is the local currency. Foreign currencies can generally be exchanged at major hotels and banks.

Language

Tigrinya, Arabic, and English are commonly spoken, so English-speaking visitors can navigate fairly easily.

Safety

Asmara is known to be a safe city for tourists, but, as with any travel, it’s wise to stay aware of your surroundings and follow local guidelines.
